What We Stitch, Print & Produce

One shop, every way to brand it.

From a single polo to a stadium's worth of jerseys, our shop handles the whole job under one roof, right here in Vallejo.

Our Specialty

Embroidery

Logos and designs stitched directly into polos, jackets, hats, and bags for a durable, professional look that holds up wash after wash.

Screen Printing

Bold, full color designs printed on t-shirts and apparel. Great for large team or event orders.

Digitizing

We turn your logo into a stitch-ready file so it embroiders cleanly every time.

Vinyl & Heat Transfers

Custom lettering and numbers applied by heat, perfect for jerseys and one-off pieces.

Dye Sublimation

Full color, edge to edge printing for performance wear and specialty fabrics.

Graphic Design

Need artwork? Our team designs or cleans up your logo before it goes into production.

Signs, Banners & Decals

Storefront signs, event banners, vehicle lettering, and decals made in house.

Business Stationery

Business cards, forms, and checks printed for your company.
