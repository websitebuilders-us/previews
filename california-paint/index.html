Fourteen Years in Fairfield

A local painting crew, not a franchise.

California Paint Company (legally LB Ford Painting Inc) has been painting homes and businesses around Fairfield and Solano County since 2012 — fourteen years of the same crew showing up, protecting your floors and furniture, and leaving rooms cleaner than we found them.

We're rated A+ with the Better Business Bureau, and every estimate is free — no pressure, no inflated first quote to "negotiate down." Interior, exterior, cabinets, or a full commercial repaint, you get the same crew from the first walkthrough to the final coat.

Based at 4325 Abernathy Rd, Fairfield, CA, we work throughout Solano County and the surrounding communities.
