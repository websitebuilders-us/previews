Our story

Forty years on Tennessee Street.

MCIA Insurance Services, LLC opened its doors in 1983 as McDowell's Insurance Agency. Four decades later, we're still an independent, locally-run agency in the heart of Vallejo — not a franchise, not a national call center.

Independent means we're not tied to one insurance company. We shop your auto, home, business, and life coverage across multiple carriers and bring back the policy that actually fits your risk and your budget.
